Malibu Rescue

rating: 9.7 / 10 year: 2017 director: Seth Gordon

Description

A group of beach lifeguards work on the Pacific coast. They are always at their post and protect vacationers from water accidents. The main character, experienced lifeguard Mitch Buchannon, and his young colleague Matt Brody accidentally learn that their beach is in danger of extinction. A big oil company is about to destroy the coast, pursuing the personal interests of management, firmly tied to finances.
